1920s Berlin

1920s Berlin was a decade of Expressionism, Bauhaus, pioneering science, drag balls, and Marlene Dietrich. A heyhey of culture, Berlin in its roaring 20s was groundbreaking in art, invention and thought. In this gallery, we have featured key works from the era, including vivid portraits by Otto Dix and Christian Schad.

Works and content adapted from Rainer Metzger Taschen art publications.
